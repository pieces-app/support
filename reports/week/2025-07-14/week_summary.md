## Common Issues
### 1. Pieces Desktop App and CLI on macOS: LTM/Workstream Activity Not Updating
Users on macOS are experiencing issues with the Pieces desktop application and CLI where LTM and Workstream Activity are not updating or recording new activities.  Issues range from complete stoppage after a short period of recording to failures in opening the Pieces Copilot service.  Error messages include "Application not found" and issues with the Gemini-pro model. Further investigation is needed to pinpoint the root cause and potential solutions.

### 2. PiecesOS integration failures across platforms
Users on Linux, macOS, and Windows report PiecesOS integration issues with desktop apps and CLI. Problems include broken workstream summaries, failure to detect Ollama on Linux, CLI model validation errors on macOS, and missing PiecesOS directories on Windows. Suggested solutions include verifying Ollama installation/path, checking PiecesOS version compatibility, and reinstalling PiecesOS/desktop apps.

### 3. Auto-Generated Context Resets to Medium
Users report that the Auto-Generated Context setting in the Pieces desktop app (macOS) repeatedly reverts to "Medium" even after being manually set to "High". This occurs across multiple builds and OS versions.  The issue may persist after app or OS updates.  Users are unsure if the "High" setting ever takes effect.
