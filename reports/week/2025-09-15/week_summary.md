Multiple users report "unauthorized request" errors when attempting to connect to Pieces Cloud across different platforms (macOS, Windows). Suggested solutions include verifying account status, checking network connectivity, disabling security software temporarily, and reinstalling/updating Pieces OS and browser extensions.
