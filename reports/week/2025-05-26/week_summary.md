Troubleshooting steps include checking for updates, restarting the application, and disabling LTM.

### 5. Pieces Desktop Issues with LTM
Users report issues with Pieces Desktop related to LTM (Long Term Memory) feature. These include incorrect date/time displayed in context, Copilot generating unrelated content despite context, application crashes or freezes, especially on macOS and Windows.  Issues seem to occur after periods of inactivity or when LTM is enabled.  Updating to the latest Pieces OS version and restarting the application are suggested troubleshooting steps.
