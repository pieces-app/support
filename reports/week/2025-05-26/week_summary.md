### 2. Pieces Desktop Performance Issues and Freezes
Users report performance issues, including slowdowns and freezes, with Pieces Desktop on macOS and Windows.  On macOS, high CPU and GPU usage is observed, especially with LTM 2 enabled. On Windows, freezes occur after periods of inactivity, particularly after interacting with chat and with LTM enabled.  Troubleshooting steps include checking for updates, restarting the application, and disabling LTM.

Users report issues with Pieces Desktop related to LTM (Long Term Memory) feature. These include incorrect date/time displayed in context, Copilot generating unrelated content despite context, application crashes or freezes, especially on macOS and Windows.  Issues seem to occur after periods of inactivity or when LTM is enabled.  Updating to the latest Pieces OS version might resolve some of these problems. Further investigation is needed to pinpoint the exact causes and solutions.
