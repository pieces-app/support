## Common Issues
### 1. Pieces OS 12.0.0 Performance and Stability Issues
Users report performance degradation and instability with Pieces OS 12.0.0 across different operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux).  Symptoms include Chrome freezes on large webpages with LTM 2.5 enabled, slow or unresponsive "New chats" feature, and application freezing during initialization.  Potential solutions include disabling LTM 2.5, checking LLM model compatibility, and reverting to a previous Pieces OS version.
